Our Mission

Founded in 2020 by Allstudio, a renowned industrial design agency, Found is a Montreal-based brand specializing in furniture, lighting and interior accessories. Our multidisciplinary team of designers, engineers and technicians remains united in its commitment to creating authentic, sustainable products designed to energize any environment.

As we strive to create unique products, we take pride in sourcing materials only from Quebec suppliers, ensuring that every element of our products is made locally, contributing to the economy and cultural richness of our region.
